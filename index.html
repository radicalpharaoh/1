Summary of this fanatastic series:

One of the wildest, most bizarre japanese series the world has ever witnessed!

Renown for being bizarre as it is amazing, the series has both book and television adaptations.

This series spans over 8 parts, with each main character nicknamed JoJo, each more bizarre than the one before!

These main characters face off against any imaginable threat, from teachers to gorillas to even vampires!

To fight all evil, the powers of each character is different, allowing for different stories and solutions to be thread.
