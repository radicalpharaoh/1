Summary of this fanatastic series:

One of the wildest, most bizarre japanese series the world has ever witnessed!

Renown for being bizarre as it is amazing, the series has both book and television adaptations.

This series spans over 8 parts, with each main character nicknamed JoJo, each more bizarre than the one before!

These main characters face off against any imaginable threat, from teachers to gorillas to even vampires!

To fight all evil, the powers of each character is different, allowing for different stories and solutions to be thread.

Villains

Something that makes JoJo's Bizarre Adventure rise above the rest of the competition is their villains!

All of JoJo's villains all have motives that don't just make them normal evil, they all trascend evil in their own way.

For instance, there are two main vampire antagonist, both belonging in different eras;

Kars, whose goal is to become the perfect being through the use of magic stones

and Dio, the eternal nemesis of the JoJo series and wants to rule the world through pure domination and manipulation.

Both of these villains are both vampires and malicious to the core, but both craft their own type of extreme evil onto the readers and watchers.
